üåΩ Make the Corn Tortillas

  • 1 cup masa harina
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water (adjust as needed)
  • Mix into a soft dough. Divide into 6 portions and roll into balls.
  • Press or roll each ball into a flat disc (~6 inch diameter).
  • Cook on a hot, dry skillet 1-2 minutes per side until lightly browned.

  • Fresh greens: lettuce, arugula, or spinach.
  • Optional extras: avocado slices, pickled onions, or shredded carrots.

üåø Assemble Your Tortilla Wrap

  1. Place a warm tortilla on a plate.
  2. Layer greens first, then protein, then vegetables.
  3. Add optional extras like avocado or pickled veggies.
  4. Top generously with üåø Zesty Cilantro-Lime Salsa.
  5. Roll or fold the tortilla and enjoy immediately!
