Ingredients (Serves 4)

  • 1 cup fresh cilantro leaves, gently packed
  • 1 can (400 ml) coconut cream
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup or agave syrup
  • Juice and zest of 2 limes
  • A pinch of sea salt
  • Optional: 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Blend cilantro, coconut cream, coconut milk, lime juice, zest, and maple syrup until smooth and bright green.
  2. Chill for at least 2 hours to let flavors deepen.
  3. If using an ice cream maker, churn per instructions. Otherwise, freeze in container, stirring every 30 min for 2‚Äì3 hours.
  4. Scoop and garnish with cilantro leaves and lime zest. Enjoy mindfully!
