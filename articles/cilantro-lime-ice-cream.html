🍃 Cilantro-Lime Ice Cream: A Cool Twist on Freshness

A refreshing plant-based dessert that celebrates creativity, calm, and contrast.

🌿 A Bright Surprise

Sometimes the most unexpected ingredients lead to the brightest flavors. This cilantro-lime ice cream is creamy, light, and delightfully refreshing — a plant-based dessert that captures the essence of summer. If you’ve ever loved the crisp, green aroma of fresh cilantro, this recipe will surprise you with how perfectly it pairs with lime and coconut cream.

🍦 Ingredients (Serves 4)

  • 1 cup fresh cilantro leaves, gently packed
  • 1 can (400 ml) coconut cream
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup or agave syrup
  • Juice and zest of 2 limes
  • A pinch of sea salt
  • Optional: 1/4 tsp vanilla extract for extra smoothness

🌀 Instructions

  1. Blend the base: In a blender, combine cilantro, coconut cream, coconut milk, lime juice, zest, and maple syrup. Blend until smooth and bright green.
  2. Chill: Pour into a bowl and refrigerate for at least 2 hours to allow flavors to deepen.
  3. Churn or freeze: If you have an ice cream maker, churn according to instructions. If not, pour into a container and freeze, stirring every 30 minutes for 2–3 hours to prevent large ice crystals.
  4. Serve mindfully: Scoop into bowls and garnish with a few cilantro leaves and a sprinkle of lime zest. Breathe in the aroma — green, cooling, alive.

🌞 Mindful Moment

This ice cream reminds us that balance often comes from contrast — cool meets bright, creamy meets herbal, familiar meets new. In the same way, mindfulness isn’t about avoiding strong feelings; it’s about blending them until something harmonious emerges.
